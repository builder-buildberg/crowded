Frequently Asked Questions

  • How to Book a slot?

    Booking a slot is easy with our platform. Simply click the "Book Now" button and you will be provided with a phone number to call. You can also search for grounds in specific locations to find the perfect place to play. Our user-friendly platform makes it easy to find and book the perfect ground for your next game.

  • What is procedure to list a ground on crowded?

    Looking to list your ground on our platform? Simply click the "Get Listed" button and a form will appear for you to fill out. Our team will then be in touch with you within 24 hours. You can also contact us directly through the email provided in the contacts section if you have any questions or need assistance. Let us help you reach more players and grow your business.

  • Is their any process for the Ground verification?

    To ensure the accuracy and credibility of our platform, our team conducts on-site visits to verify the existence of the listed grounds and assess the available facilities. This ensures that users can trust the information provided on our platform and have confidence in their bookings.

  • How can we contact with you?

    If you have any questions or need assistance, don't hesitate to reach out to us. You can contact us through the email or phone number provided in the contacts section. Our team is always happy to help.
